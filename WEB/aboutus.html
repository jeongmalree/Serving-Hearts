About Us

Our Story

Serving Hearts Charity Inc. was established in 2020 in response to the declining blood supply in the Pasig area. Recognizing the urgent need for a stable blood supply, especially during the COVID-19 pandemic, a group of dedicated individuals came together to form this charitable organization.

Our mission is to provide care and support for individuals willing to donate blood. We operate on a unique membership model, fostering a community of committed donors.

By encouraging voluntary blood donations and ensuring the blood reaches those in need, Serving Hearts plays a crucial role in saving lives and supporting healthcare facilities in the region.

Through community engagement, we continue to raise awareness about the importance of blood donation, making a real difference in people's lives.
