Eligibility Criteria

Criteria Details
Age 16-65 years (parental consent required for donors under 18)
Weight At least 50 kg (110 lbs) for a 450 ml donation
Pulse Rate 60-100 beats per minute
Blood Pressure 90-160 mmHg systolic and 60-100 mmHg diastolic
Hemoglobin 125 g/l or 0.37
Tattoo Must be at least one year since the last tattoo
Piercing Must be at least one year since the last piercing

Before Donating Blood

Complete a confidential medical history form. Review our guidelines to ensure eligibility.

  • No recent use of injected drugs or substances not prescribed by a doctor
  • No new sexual partners or risky behaviors in the past three months
  • No positive HIV test or history of certain diseases

Benefits of Donating Blood

Your donation saves lives and provides personal benefits. It can:

  • Reduce stress
  • Enhance emotional well-being
  • Benefit your physical health
