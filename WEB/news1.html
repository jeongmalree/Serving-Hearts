OUR BLOOD, a LIFELINE for OTHERS
Heartfelt thanks to all blood donors for giving the gift of life.

Event Summary

We are pleased to share that the bloodletting activity held at the Silangan Covered Court, Silangan Village, Geronimo, Rodriguez, Rizal, on November 3, 2024, was a great success. The event ran from 7:00 AM to 2:00 PM and saw an overwhelming response from the community.

Organized by Serving Hearts Charity Inc., the event was supported by local volunteers and community partners who helped make the day a success. A large number of donors participated, offering their blood as a lifeline to those in need.

We extend our sincere gratitude to all donors, volunteers, and partners who contributed to this cause. Your generosity will have a lasting impact, helping save countless lives.

If you couldn’t attend, we hope you’ll join us in future bloodletting events and continue supporting our mission to provide life-saving blood to those in need.
