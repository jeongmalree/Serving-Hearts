Upcoming Blood Donation Event

We are thrilled to announce our upcoming blood donation event that will take place on August 3, 2024. This is a wonderful opportunity for the community to come together and support a vital cause.

Event Summary

We are excited to share that the bloodletting activity held at the Senior Citizen Pavilion, San Bernardo Street, San Joaquin, Pasig City, on November 17, 2024, was a tremendous success. The event ran from 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM and saw an enthusiastic response from the community.

Serving Hearts Charity Inc. organized this life-saving event, with dedicated volunteers and participants coming together to support the cause. Many generous donors contributed, helping ensure a steady supply of blood for those in need.

We are deeply grateful to all the donors, volunteers, and partners who made this event possible. Your support and contributions will help save many lives and strengthen our community’s bond.

If you couldn’t join us this time, we encourage you to participate in future events and continue supporting our mission of saving lives through blood donation.
