"Blood Donors Praised for Life-Saving Contributions in Maybunga, Pasig City."

Event Summary

We are proud to announce the success of the bloodletting activity held on October 27, 2024, at the Phase 1 Bankers Village V Multipurpose Covered Court, San Mateo, Rizal. The event ran from 8:00 AM to 2:00 PM and was met with a remarkable turnout from the local community.

Organized by Serving Hearts Charity Inc., the event saw many selfless donors come forward to contribute to this life-saving cause. Their generosity will help provide critical blood supplies for those in need.

We extend our heartfelt thanks to all the blood donors, volunteers, and partners for making this event possible. Your contributions have made a profound difference and will help save countless lives.

For those who missed this event, we encourage you to join us in future bloodletting activities and continue supporting our mission to provide the gift of life through blood donation.
